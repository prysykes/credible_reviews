Frequently Asked Questions

Most Questions have already been answered elaboratively on How to Use page. Kindly visit it, you may find answers to your curiosity...

How can I invite my customers to Review my company on this site
Kindly login to your Company User Account, on your profile page, click on 'Send Review Request to a Customer', enter the customers email and click send. The customer will receive an automated email and a link to our site to review your service.
How do I send a private message to a business listed here
Please login to your account as a Regular User, search for the company using its name on the search bar above. Once you see the company from the list, click it. You will be directed to the company page. You can now send your message there. You must be logged in as a Regular User to do this
Do you influence Reviews
Credible Reviews will never influence customer Reviews for any company. This is to ensure equity and transparency which are the core values of our Company.
How did my company end up here

Your organization is currently listed on Credible Reviews because someone thought their experience with your company was worth sharing with the general public. We also carry out due diligence and do our best to ensure Reviews are accurate and really a heartfelt experience. If the information about your company is outdated or you need it improved, kindly Contact Us.

Having your company listed here is an advantage. You can enjoy the full benefits of Credible Reviews by signing up as a Company User and claming the company. This enables you to respond to reviews from Users, receive and respond to private messages, increase sales, get seen by our millions of daily Users, manage your page and much more. Please visit our How to Use page to find out more.

What if I disagree with a Review of my Company
If you feel that a Review is false or fabricated by a competitor, kindly email us on: abuse@crediblereviews.ng with the username of the reviewer, subject and content of the review. We will take appropriate actions once this is received.
Are there any hidden cost
All the services at Credible Reviews are FREE.
How can I get my business listed
Please Sign Up as a Company User, search the site using the search bar above to find your company. If your company is already listed, kindly write us to link the company to your account. Else, use the link in your profile to list your company for free. Our How to Use page has more details abouts this.
How much does it cost to advertise here
Kindly send an email to sales@crediblereviews.ng for details, offers and promotions on how to advertise on our site.
What is the blog section about
Our Blog is for general discussion on the marketing space. You are also able to discover exotic places, businesses and products by visiting our blog. We also provide tips on online safety, predator companies, fake or bad products and businesses.
Any guideline or rules for posting reviews and listing businesses
Firstly, we are in no Way represented by any category of User on this site hence, their reviews and opinions are exclusively theirs and not in any way the opinion or view of Credible Reviews. You are not allowed to post political and illicit contents like (fraudulent or misguiding reviews and pornographic contents). Failure to stick to this rule will get your account banned!
