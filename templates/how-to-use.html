How To Use

Reviews must be a sincere and personal experience. Anything short of this will get your account disabled. Please, Credible Reviews cannot influence reviews.

There are two categories of Users with different privileges on the site. Please choose one that best suits your needs.


  • Regular Users
  • Company Users
  • Click either of the links to Sign Up now...

Regular Users - You can review a company, send private messages to a company, edit your reviews and private messages.

Company Users - After creating this account, you can search the site to determine if your company is already listed on our site. If it is, you can request for it to be linked to your account else, you can list your company from your profile. There is a link for that there. Once you have linked your account to a company, you are now able to respond to reviews and private messages for that company.

Private messages are as the name implies, Private. They can not be seen by any other user or the general public except the company they are meant for.

Reviews are Public and can be seen by all categories of users and the general public (whether logged in or not).

Please ensure you adopt one that best suits the situation. Thank you.

To send a review, you must be logged in as a Regular User. You can review a company by searching, clicking on it and sending a sincere review on the company's page.

You can also click the submit review button once you're signed in, type the company name and send your sincere review. If the company does not appear, kindly select other, provide details of the company and send your review.

Company Users are not allowed to review a company. This is to ensure equity and transparency in the overall review process.

Company Users are only allowed to view and respond to reviews associated with their company alone. They are also able to respond to private messages from their customers on the site.

To respond to reviews for a particular company, Company Users MUST have linked that company to their account if the company already exist on our site or list the company from their profile page.

Once you list your company, you will get a notification that it is awaiting approval. This is to enable us review your listing and ensure accurate information is provided. Your listing will be approved once this validation process is over.

Once approved, Regular Users can now find the company on our site to review. You are also now able to respond to reviews and private messages for this company.

Regular Users - Sending Reviews and Private Messages is totally FREE. We expect absolute sincerity when writing reviews.

All Company Users are automatically placed on Premium package once verified. This implies that you can enjoy all the benefits of Credible Reviews at no cost. You can list a company, ask your customers for Reviews by sending them an automated email, recieve and respond to private messages, receive and respond to Reviews on your company by our users all at no cost.

Credible Reviews will not and cannot influence customers reviews on your page hence, you are advised to respond to these reviews as soon as they come. To enable you biuld your reputation on the site and the business world in general.
